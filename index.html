About CodeSenso

CodeSenso, aka CodeWar, aims to engage a combination of talents in this Inter-College technical event.

We seek your support in spreading the word to your Coding Community and would be grateful for the same.

A two round event in which participants have to write the codes to the problems presented to them in the given time, keeping in mind the efficiency and memory usage of program.

This event gives coders the opportunity to meet other enthusiasts like themselves, and push boundaries. From apprentices to the pros, you’ll find a nice mix of skills and ideas.

FAQ


1Is it necessary to have CodeChef account to partcipate in the competition?

Yes, Coders are required to have a CodeChef account in order to partcipate in the contest.

2Is it compulsory to bring personal laptops to participate.

Coders may or may not bring their personal laptop. However, it is recommended to bring your own laptop. Computers are limited, and will be assigned on a first come, first served basis.

3Is food and accomodation facilities included in the registration fees?

Lunch will be provided after the completion of the first round. No accomodation facilities will be provided for partcipants.

4When will the contest start and how much time will it take?

The competition will start at 9:30am and there will be two rounds. Each round will be 2hr 30 min long. The two rounds will end by 6 pm lately. Coders who clear the first round will be eligible to partcipate in the second round.

5What are the payment methods available

We will accept payment via Paytm only, whose QR code is provided in the google form.
